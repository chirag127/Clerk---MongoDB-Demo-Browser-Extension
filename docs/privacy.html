Introduction

ClerkAuth MongoDB Data Browser Extension ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

•
Authentication Data:

We use Clerk Authentication to securely manage your login sessions. Your authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser.

•
Database Connection Details:

MongoDB connection strings and credentials are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

•
Usage Analytics:

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension experience. No personal data is included.

•
Error Logs:

Anonymous error reports are automatically sent to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • ✓ To provide and maintain the Extension's functionality
  • ✓ To authenticate users and manage secure sessions
  • ✓ To connect to your MongoDB databases for browsing data
  • ✓ To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • ✓ To debug issues and maintain service quality
